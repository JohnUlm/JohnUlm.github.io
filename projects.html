Fully Autonomous Robot Project

FEB 23 - APR 23

I worked in a team of four to create a fully autonomous robot over one semester. The robot was coded in C and featured the use of optosensor line-following, CdS cells, bump switches, and shaft encoding to navigate. I primarily worked on electrical connections, budgeting, and SolidWorks design although I contributed to construction and coding.
